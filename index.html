Agent 47

Who is Agent 47?

47, known by very few as Tobias Rieper, is a paid assassin and a clone. He gets his name from being the 47th clone created from the DNA of five different men, made to be perfect assassins who could easily maintain peak physicality and intelligence while easily being controlled by whoever they were devoted to, usually a handler.

47 Lives a lonely life

A life of solitude...

  1. He's got no friends
  2. He's got no family
  3. He's got nothing to lose

Why live then?

While 47 doesn't have any friends or biological family, he does have his the clones who came before him and after him, some of which he grew up with, facing torment, gas-lighting and wreckless training.
